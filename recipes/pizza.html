Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a round flatbread topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, etc. (as desired)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220Â°C.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough and place it on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough and add cheese and toppings.
  4. Bake in the oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
