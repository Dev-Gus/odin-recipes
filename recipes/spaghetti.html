Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian dish featuring pasta served with a savory tomato sauce and topped with grated Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
