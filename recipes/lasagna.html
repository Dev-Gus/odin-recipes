Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish consisting of layers of pasta intermingled with meat sauce and béchamel.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 200g ricotta cheese
  • 100g grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan until browned.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes and simmer.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Layer in a baking dish: meat sauce, lasagna sheets, cheese mixture. Repeat.
  5. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
